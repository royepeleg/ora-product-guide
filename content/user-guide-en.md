 <img

      style="max-width:40%; height: auto; border-radius: 12px; box-shadow: 0 4px 14px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.15)" />

 <img

 <img
      src="/images/sddoor1.jpg"

  <li>Gently ensure the door is fully closed and the propeller can rotate freely</li>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 2 – Power On</p>

<p>Turn on the drone</p>
<p>Turn on the controller</p>
<p>Turn on the goggles</p>
<p>Wait for the full startup sequence until the drone video feed appears on the screen</p>

<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 3 – System Operation</p>

<p>Press the photo button on the controller — after ~5 seconds the lights will turn on</p>
 <img
      src="/images/photobtn.jpg"
      alt="Product Image"
      style="max-width:40%; height: auto; border-radius: 12px; box-shadow: 0 4px 14px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.15)" />

<p>Turn the system off and on twice to verify proper operation</p>

<p>- You may turn the drone off and on during flight  
<br>
</p>
- Be sure to wait for the flashlight to turn on before pressing again to turn it off - and vice versa.

<hr/>
