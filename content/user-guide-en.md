<p style="font-size: 1.7rem; font-weight: 700; margin-top: 0;">Operating Manual</p>

<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 1 – Pre-Flight Checks</p>

<p>Check the rear unit connection to the drone</p>
Image

<p>Check the battery connection to the rear unit</p>
Image

<p>Check that the front unit is seated properly and has no play</p>
Image

<p>Check the black cable connection to the bottom of the front light</p>
Image

<p>Check the memory-card door on the drone</p>
<ul>
  <li>Gently ensure the door is fully closed and the propeller can rotate freely</li>
  <li>Ensure the flexible cable exits the door straight without bending</li>
</ul>
Image

<p>Check the memory card connection to the rear unit  
<br>Make sure the connector is fully inserted so it does not enter the propeller area during flight</p>
Image

<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 2 – Power On</p>

<p>Turn on the drone</p>
<p>Turn on the controller</p>
<p>Turn on the goggles</p>
<p>Wait for the full startup sequence until the drone video feed appears on the screen</p>

<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 3 – System Operation</p>

<p>Press the photo button on the controller — after ~5 seconds the lights will turn on</p>

<p>Turn the system off and on twice to verify proper operation</p>

<p>You may turn the drone off and on during flight  
<br>Make sure to wait for full startup before shutting down — and vice versa</p>

<hr/>
