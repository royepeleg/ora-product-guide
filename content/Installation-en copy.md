<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 1 – Insert the Memory Card</p>

<p>
Insert the memory card into the slot located inside the left-rear propeller guard  
(as shown in the picture), on the drone-side inner wall.
</p>
<img src="/images/cdslot1.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />
<img src="/images/cddoor2.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />

<p>
Insert the card gently with the label facing downward.  
Guide the flexible cable upward toward the top of the drone.  
Close the card cover gently and ensure the propeller rotates freely.
</p>


<img src="/images/cddoor1.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />
<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 2 – Secure the Memory Cable</p>

<p>
Secure the connector of the memory card cable to the drone body using the clip,  
ensuring it does not interfere with the propeller.
</p>

<img src="/images/cdconclips1.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />
<img src="/images/cdcon1.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />

<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 3 – Connect the Rear Unit</p>

<p>
Connect the rear module to the battery interface on the drone's body.  
It is recommended to perform the first installation without the battery.
</p>

<p style=" font-weight:700;">
Important: Insert it straight to avoid bending the pins.
</p>

<img src="/images/rearbefor.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />

<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 4 – Connect the Front Cable</p>

<p>
Connect the black Type-C cable to the front light module of the kit,  
</p>
<img src="/images/rearafter2.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />
<img src="/images/frontcable.jpg"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />

<p>
then snap the front clip into place as shown.
</p>
<img src="/images/frontclips.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />
<img src="/images/front.JPG"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />



<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 5 – Connect the Memory Cable</p>

<p>
Connect the memory card cable to the matching connector coming from the drone.
</p>

<img src="/images/sdconnection.jpg"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />

<hr/>

<p style="font-weight:600; font-size:1.5rem;">Step 6 – Install the Battery</p>

<p>
Insert the battery into its slot and connect it to the kit.
</p>
<img src="/images/btrcon.jpg"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />
<img src="/images/btrcon2.jpg"
     style="max-width:40%; height:auto; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,.15);" />

<p>
After completing the installation, follow the testing and activation steps  
described in the  
<a href="/user-guide-en/" style="color:#007bff; font-weight:600;">User Guide</a>.
</p>
